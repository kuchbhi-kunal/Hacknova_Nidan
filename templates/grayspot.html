Disease Diagnosed

Cercospora Leaf Gray Spot


Solution

Grey leaf spot (GLS) is a foliar fungal disease that affects maize, also known as corn. GLS is considered one of the most significant yield-limiting diseases of corn worldwide. The fungus survives in the debris of topsoil and infects healthy crops via asexual spores called conidia In order to best prevent and manage corn grey leaf spot following solution can be made:

  • 1. Susceptible hybrid
  • 2. Continuous corn
  • 3. Late planting date
  • 4. Minimum tillage systems
  • 5. Field history of severe disease
  • 6. Early disease activity (before tasseling)
  • 7. Irrigation
  • 8. Favorable weather forecast for disease
