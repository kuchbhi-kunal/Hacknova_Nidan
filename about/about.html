Corn

Sweet corn is generally free of serious diseases. Occasional a grayblack mass appears on the ears. It is caused by a fungal disease known as smut. The best control is to cut off the ear and dispose of it.

Grapes

The Crop is suspect to diseases like downy mildew, powdery mildew, black rot, wilt, leaf blight etc. Timely treatment and control measures are needed. Some of the disorders observed in case of grapes are post harvest berry drop, berry cracking, leaf cholorosis, dead arm & trunk splitting.

Potatoes

There are many diseases that affect potato tubers, so as you sort through your potato harvest each year, take a moment to check for disease symptoms. Proper identification will help you decide which tubers will store well and which should be sold as tablestock.
